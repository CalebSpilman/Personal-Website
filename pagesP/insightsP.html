Business Literature: Gary Vaynerchuk's "Crushing It!""

I found this book to be a valuable resource regarding it's alignment with foundational understandings of business optimization and the value of systems analysis, in that it emphasizes the strategic use of digital ecosystems. From an independent consulting perspective, the book underscores valuable principles about the importance of a strategic digital presence, and agile approach to brand-building, which not only aligns with how one can market themselves to clients (like an independent consultant such as myself), but also with how they organizations can restructure their business models digitally to more efficiently position themselves. This book is very telling of the digital environment modern businesses are operating in, and a good reminder that sustainable growth and operation are rooted in being able to position your brand and business model to adapt to the rapidly growing environment of technology.
